Do’s

 Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
 Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
 Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
 Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
 Engage positively and respectfully with others online.


 Don’ts

 Do not share sensitive personal details (address, phone number, passwords).
 Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
 Do not click on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources.
 Avoid oversharing your location or daily routine publicly.
 Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.

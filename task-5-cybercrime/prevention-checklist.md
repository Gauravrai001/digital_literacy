 Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)
 
 Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts; avoid using the same password everywhere.                                                                        
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on apps like email, social media, and banking.                                                                             
 Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or company.                                                 
 Be careful while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or important accounts on unsecured networks.                                                       
 Verify links and messages before clicking—scammers often send fake job offers, discounts, or urgent alerts.                                                       
 Keep your phone and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.                                                                                      
 Review your social media privacy settings and avoid oversharing personal information.                                                                                   
 

 UPI / Financial Safety Tips:        
    
 Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only required to send money.                                                                                       
 Always double-check the receiver’s name before approving any payment request.                                                                                  
 Avoid scanning unknown QR codes or accepting payment requests from strangers.                                                                                        

 If you face cyber fraud:                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                             
Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in                                                                                           
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for quick assistance)
